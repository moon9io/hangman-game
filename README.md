A clean, feature-rich, and fully responsive browser-based **Hangman Game** built with pure ** Vanilla JavaScript, HTML5, and CSS3**. Experience the classic word-guessing game with a modern touch and full support for both Arabic and English languages.

![ Hangman Game Preview ](https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1550741 served correctly with local assets if available)

## ✨ Key Features

- **🌍 Bilingual Support**: Seamlessly switch between **Arabic** and **English** interfaces.
- **🌓 Theme Toggle**: Choice between **Dark Mode** and **Light Mode** for comfortable gameplay.
- **🏆 Achievement System**: 10 unique achievements to unlock as you play.
- **💰 Economy & Hints**: Earn points for correct guesses and use them to buy hints.
- ** the difficulty Levels**: Choose between Easy, Medium, and Hard modes to test your skills.
- **🎵 procedural Audio**: Immersive sound effects generated via Web Audio API.
- **💾 Persistent Progress**: Your high scores and achievements are saved locally in your browser.

## 🛠 Technology Stack

- **HTML5**: Semantic structure.
- **CSS3**: Custom properties (variables), Flexbox, and Grid.
- **JavaScript (ES6+)**: Logic, state management, and DOM manipulation.
- **Web Audio API**: Real- time sound synthesis.

## 🎮 How to Play

1. ** Guess the Word**: You'll see a row of underscores representing the hidden word.
2. ** Pick a Letter**: Use the on-screen keyboard or your physical keyboard to guess a letter.
3. ** Watch the Gallows**: Each incorrect guess adds a part to the hangman drawing.
4. ** Win or Lose**: Guess the word before the drawing is complete to win!
